A Beard Of Bees

A beard of bees is an indie folk band with Indonesian roots. They try to use Indonesian elements in their music to represent their culture. They get their inspiration from other indie folk bands like the fleet foxes. They started in locas cafes and local festivals, but now they are one of the big names at festivals like Rock Werchter.

Maurice Mossley

Maurice Mossley is a young American upcoming talent. He grew up around the coasts of Sante Barbara where he lived a peacefull life with his parents. His environment wasn’t interested in music so everybody was surprised by his musical talent. He started in small pubs but currently he’s touring around the world. His 4th album recently came out on Parenco Records.

Anastasia Veneda

Anastasia Veneda is a female singer-songwriter. She was born in Russia where she had to work in rural areas at an early age in terrible circumstances. Because of this she was not allowed to go to school, unlike most boys. When Anastasia became pregnant, she fled to Belgium in the hope of a better life. In her self-written songs she stands up for women's rights by always leaving a personal message. With support of her violin she brings intense but at the same time cheerful songs. Her soft voice, energetic footwork and overwhelming violin work are the key to her success. Anastasia will soon release her fourth album on Parenco Records.
